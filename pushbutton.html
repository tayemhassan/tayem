Meet the Push Button 🖲✨

🎮 A push button is a *tiny switch* – press it, and it completes a circuit. Simple as that! 🔄

📥 It’s an INPUT device – it sends a signal to the Arduino when pressed. 🙌

🔧 You can use it to trigger LEDs 💡, control motors ⚙, start timers ⏱, or even launch a robot dance party! 🕺🤖

🧠 The Arduino detects whether it's pressed or not using digitalRead(). HIGH means unpressed, LOW means pressed (if wired with pull-down). 🧲
