Let’s Wire It Up! 🔌💪

Connecting the LCD I2C is super simple! Only 4 wires and you're good to go 👇

  • GND ➡ GND (ground connection)
  • VCC ➡ 5V (power supply)
  • SDA ➡ A4 (Data line on Uno)
  • SCL ➡ A5 (Clock line on Uno)

🛠 Use jumper wires to make the connections. Be gentle – those pins are tiny!

🔍 There’s a small screw on the back to control brightness (contrast). Use a screwdriver to adjust.
