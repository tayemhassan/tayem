Parts & Components

Below is a detailed list of what you need for the 2-motor Bluetooth robot car (recommended parts + notes):

Core electronics

  • Arduino Uno / Nano — main controller. Nano is smaller and fits the chassis.
  • Bluetooth Module (HC-05 / HC-06) — serial wireless link; HC-05 can be master/slave.
  • Motor Driver (L298N or L293D) — drives two DC motors and accepts direction and PWM inputs.
  • 2 × DC Geared Motors — pair matched for similar RPM/torque (12V or 6V depending on driver & battery).

Power & mechanical

  • Battery pack — e.g., 2S Li-ion (7.4V) or 6×AA NiMH depending on motors.
  • Chassis kit — base plate, wheel hubs, caster wheel.
  • Wires, connectors, screws — male/female jumpers, terminal blocks.

Optional (recommended)

  • Small breadboard or perfboard for tidy wiring
  • Voltage regulator (if L298N onboard regulator unsuitable)
  • Indicator LEDs and resistors for power/mode signals
