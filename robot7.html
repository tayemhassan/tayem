Bluetooth Control, Troubleshooting & Enhancements

Bluetooth pairing & app

- Pair the HC-05 with your phone (default PIN often 1234 or 0000).
- Use a serial-Bluetooth terminal app or a custom controller app (many “Bluetooth Controller” apps on Play Store).

Typical command flow

  • App sends 'F' → HC-05 receives → Arduino executes forward()
  • App sends 'L' → Arduino executes turnLeft()
  • Send 'S' to stop

Troubleshooting tips

  • No response: check HC-05 power & wiring, ensure common ground.
  • Motors jitter: battery low or noisy — add decoupling capacitors and use proper battery.
  • HC-05 not pairing: check module state LED; ensure correct voltage on RX (use divider).

Enhancements

- Add ultrasonic sensor for obstacle avoidance (automate stop/turn).
- Add speed control slider in the app and pass numeric speed values to Arduino.
- Add LED front/back indicators and battery voltage monitor.
