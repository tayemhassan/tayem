Wiring & Full Circuit

This section shows the full wiring between Arduino, HC-05, L298N, the two motors and the battery.

Summary of connections

Bluetooth HC-05:

  • HC-05 VCC → Arduino 5V (or external 5V)
  • HC-05 GND → Arduino GND
  • HC-05 TX → Arduino RX (or SoftwareSerial RX pin)
  • HC-05 RX → Arduino TX (via voltage divider to step Arduino TX to ~3.3V)

L298N & Motors:

  • L298N Vmotor → Battery positive (7.4V / as required)
  • L298N GND → Battery negative & Arduino GND (common ground)
  • IN1,IN2 → Arduino digital pins (e.g., 8,9) for motor A
  • EN A → Arduino PWM pin (e.g., 10)
  • IN3,IN4 → Arduino digital pins (e.g., 4,5) for motor B
  • EN B → Arduino PWM pin (e.g., 11)
  • OUT pins → Motor terminals

Wiring diagram (example)

Important: always connect grounds together (battery negative, Arduino GND, L298N GND). If L298N has an onboard 5V regulator and you want to power Arduino from motor supply, follow module documentation and caution.
